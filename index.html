ITS Division

TATA Iron and Steel Company, Jamshedpur

Automated the process of capturing real time Slab Casting information for the Slab Casting division (SMPL). The manual process consisted of 8 CRT terminals with real time running marquees reporting the composition of the steel being cast, which was noted down manually by 12 individuals per shift on a 2 feet by 2 feet sized 'HEAT LOG Sheet'. The process was cumbersome and error prone, more specifically, prone to errors of omission since the specificity of 118 mineral constituents of steel were logged for every batch of steel being cast.

The new automated technology eliminated manual intervention completely and captured the data real time with the help of Korean sensors installed in the steel caster injectors to report back, casting composition on a real time basis. This information was captured using the ORACLE 'C' interface in the form of C data structures. The captured data was stored into the central ORACLE database repository for future use by Quality Assurance personnel in the form of formatted reports designed to their suitability.
This resulted in the end to end implementation of Automated Caster Parameter Tracking System (ACPTS).
